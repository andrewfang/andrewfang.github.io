Around

Discover and Create New Moments Around You

Discover

Explore the area around you through Instagram photos.

Create

Take and draw on new photos using a wide variety of brush colors. Save or share on your favorite social media accounts.

Personalize

Log onto Instagram for a personalized experience.

Built for iOS

Around was built purely for learning purposes, so it include as many iOS APIs as I could shove in. Camera, Gestures, Motions, 3DTouch, MapKit, Location, Drawing, Animations, they're all here! It will soon be on the App Store (hopefully). If you have an Apple Watch, a companion app is also available.

Discover - Feed

Scroll to see beautiful pictures taken around you in a simplified, clutter free layout. Or explore the area through a map view with pins indicating photo events.

Discover - Photo

Pinch, pan, force touch, or tilt your device to interact with the photo. View the post in a custom webview, or share it with your social network!

Create - Photo

Take a photo using either camera on your device. Share it through your favorite apps, save the photo into your camera roll, or shake the device to take a new photo.

Create - Draw

Before you share, you can draw on your photo using brushes of all different colors. Undo any color changes or brush marks with the tap of a button.

Explore

After you're done exploring your area, explore the world. Tap the current location to bring up a popover and long press on the map to choose a new location.

Watch

Get a quick glimpse of images posted around you on your Apple Watch. Because why not.
